About Us

Lucis AI is a service designed to enhance decision-making regarding your health. It leverages advanced Artificial Intelligence technologies to provide clear and reliable diagnostic interpretations for skin conditions. Our goal is to empower individuals to monitor their health proactively and take timely action when necessary. While Lucis AI provides valuable insights, it is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or advice. We are committed to privacy, security, and delivering user-friendly tools that make health monitoring accessible to everyone.

Melanoma early detection matters

Early melanoma detection is critical. It significantly increases the chances of successful treatment and long-term survival. Early-detected melanomas are smaller and easier to remove, reducing the need for invasive surgery and improving outcomes. It also lowers overall healthcare costs.
